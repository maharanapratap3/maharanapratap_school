About Our School

Established in 2001, Maharana Pratap Higher Secondary School is committed to providing quality education from Nursery to 12th grade under the MP Board curriculum. Our mission is to nurture young minds with a perfect blend of academic excellence and moral values.

Principal's Message

“Success comes to those who work hard and stays with those, who don’t rest on the laurels of the past.”

We believe that education is the most powerful tool with which one can change the world and it has been our earnest endeavor to pursue excellence in the field of academics.

Education is the movement from the darkness of slavery to the freedom of light. Hence, education and freedom go hand in hand by transforming the lives of both the teachers and the students.

Guru Nanak International School’s mission is to give the students the all-round capabilities including creativity, observation and knowledge empowerment leading to the generation of excellent performing citizens with sterling character.
